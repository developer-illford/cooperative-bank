About Our Bank

Taliparamba Co-Op. Urban Bank Ltd
Since 1996 Secure.

Taliparamba Co-Operative Urban Bank Ltd was incorporated on 24/04/1996 and commenced operations on 28/04/1996. The bank functions under the license and supervision of the Reserve Bank of India, delivering reliable banking solutions to individuals, businesses, and communities. With a strong presence in Taliparamba and surrounding regions, the bank is committed to financial inclusion, secure transactions, and customer-centric services.

Common Questions

When Can I Open a Savings Account?

You can open a savings account with us anytime with minimum documentation. Our team ensures the process is quick, secure, and hassle-free.

What Services Does a Co-op Urban Bank Provide?

We offer a wide range of services including savings and current accounts, fixed deposits, personal and business loans, financial advisory, and digital banking facilities to help manage your money efficiently.

Who Can Become a Member of the Bank?

Membership is open to residents of the local community, including individuals, small businesses, and cooperative groups, fostering a shared growth approach.

How Do I Apply for a Loan?

Our loan application process is simple: submit the required documents, meet eligibility criteria, and our advisors will guide you step-by-step to ensure timely approval.
